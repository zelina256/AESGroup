AES GROUP shpk is an Albanian registered company specialized in Architectural & Engineering Design, Project Management, Supervision, and Consulting service. The company has over 5 years of experience in the sector.

In addition to design, AES GROUP shpk provides consulting services in the preparation of tender documents, project management, construction supervision, quality control, expert appraisal, bid evaluation, and post auditing

Mission

"AES GROUP" aims to become a successful company in Albania and in the Region in the field of design study and supervision as well as in project management.

Quality, seriousness, and professionalism in offering services are at the center of our attention. The mission of "AES GROUP" is to create added value in the long term for the Client and its investments, providing high-quality services in all areas of design including, transport, infrastructure, and civil construction.

Vision

Our vision is that by institutionalizing the culture of quality to bring in the market a unique product in its kind. By doing our job with seriousness, correctly, and by respecting high standards of service allow us to create sincere, professional, and long-term relationships with our clients and partners.

Our vision is to increase efficiency, maximize sustainability, and advance with the best practices to optimize project Costs, reduce construction time and risk as well.
