"AES GROUP shpk" is an Albanian registered company specialized in Architectural & Engineering Design, Project Management, Supervision and Consulting service. The company has over 5 years of experience in the sector. In addition to design, AES GROUP shpk provides consulting services in preparation of tender documents, project management, construction supervision, quality control, expert appraisal, bid evaluation and post auditing

Mission

"AES GROUP" aims to become a successful company in Albania and in the Region in the field of design study and supervision as well as in project management. Quality, seriousness and professionalism in offering services are at the center of our attention. The mission of “AES GROUP” is to create an added value in long term for the Client and its investments, providing high quality services in all areas of design including, transport, infrastructure and civil construction.

Vision

Our vision is that by institutionalizing the culture of quality to bring in the market a product which is unique in its kind. By doing our job with seriousness, correctly and by respecting high standards of service allows us to create sincere, professional and long term relationships with our clients and partners.
